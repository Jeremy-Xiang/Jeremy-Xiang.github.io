Background

Built in one week.

This project was built during a one-week intensive at AI Camp in 2022. Starting from scratch with Python, we sourced a stroke dataset from Kaggle, trained and benchmarked eight machine learning models, and shipped this site — all in seven days. Our final AdaBoost model hit 97.3% accuracy, one of the highest recorded for this dataset.
